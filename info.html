Liziqi is a food and country life blogger who gained fame for her unique approach of filming. Li started posting her videos on Meipai in 2015. Initially, Li made her videos all by herself, her amateurish video editing skills failed to "capture the creativity" she tried to express. In 2016, one of Li's videos titled Peach Wine caught the attention of a video-making platform CEO, who featured the video on the platform's front page, which soon elicited more followers for Li's channel. As of October 2020, she has 13 million subscribers on YouTube, over 26.3 million followers on Sina Weibo, over 3.8 million followers on Facebook, and has inspired many bloggers to post similar content.

Her mainland audience includes urban millennials. Li's popularity may be attributed to fugu (复古), a growing appreciation in modern China for traditional culture. In an interview with Goldthread in September 2019, Li stated "I simply want people in the city to know where their food comes from."
